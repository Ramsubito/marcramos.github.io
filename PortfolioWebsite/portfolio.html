About

3D Environment Artist with a technical mindset, focused on the intersection between visual craft and real-time performance. My work spans cinematic environments in Unreal Engine, procedural materials in Substance Designer, and shader workflows built for both fidelity and optimization.

I care about what happens under the hood — blending pipelines, texel density, draw calls, LODs — because that's where a scene becomes shippable. Available for freelance projects and full-time positions.
